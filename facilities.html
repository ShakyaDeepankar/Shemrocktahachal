Karate

Dive into the world of discipline and physical fitness with our Karate program. Students learn essential self-defense techniques, build strength, and cultivate a sense of focus and respect.

Field Visit

Explore the wonders of the world beyond textbooks through our engaging field visits like zoo, old age home,museum. These trips not only enhance academic learning but also provide students with real-world experiences, sparking curiosity and broadening their perspectives.

Music Classes

Harmonize with creativity in our Music Classes, where students discover the joy of melody and rhythm. From playing instruments to understanding musical theory, our program nurtures a love for music and self-expression.

Dance Classes

Move and groove in our Dance Classes, where students express themselves through the art of dance. From traditional forms to contemporary styles, our program encourages creativity, boosts confidence, and promotes physical well-being.
