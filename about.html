About us

Welcome to Our School

SHEMROCK Little Harvard (Tahachal, Ward No. 13, Kathmandu, Nepal) is one of the leading preschool in Kathmandu, Renowned for its commitment to early childhood education, SHEMROCK Little Harvard stands as a leading institution dedicated to nurturing young minds. With a rich legacy, SHEMROCK Little Harvard has been a trailblazer in providing a comprehensive range of programs designed for preschool and elementary kindergarten education.

It has spacious classrooms and learning corners with hazel-free walk-through corridors and steps. Individualized learning hubs, common activity zones, and well-equipped sports facilities ensure that children develop holistically to their highest potential-whether it is academically, cognitively, emotionally, creatively, or socially.

We believe that a happy learning environment will create a happy human who will uphold values with a scientific mind. Our community strives in understanding our learners, building on their strengths, and making them lead with responsibility. We sustain their curiosity by encouraging them towards independent research and motivating them to explore for we believe that thinking minds will lead the future.
